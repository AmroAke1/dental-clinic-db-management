# dental-clinic-db-management
A database management system(DBMS) designed for a dental clinic featuring 3NF normalization.
This database is designed for an appointment and patient registration for a
dental clinic, the system will store and manage patients names, age, gender,
ID numbers, mobile number, their appointment date and time and their
payment status and each payment includes the total amount and the date it
was issued, manage staff members nurses and doctors available and their
registered patients. This system will allow front desk to register new patients
collect their details. Each appointment record is linked to one staff member
according to his/her availability it also includes the reason of visit and the
status of the appointment (scheduled, delayed or cancelled) this database
helps keep all dental clinics operations, appointments and financial data
organized
